Hi there!

Congratulations - you've reached SBHSTimetable.tk v2.0!

What's new?

  1. Security — Now you don't have to type in your timetable - or even give us your identity. All we get is a single token that allows us to retrieve data on your behalf and display it to you.
    We can't store or log your data, so only you and the people sitting behind you can see it.
  2. Simplicity — It's been rewritten from the ground up using the same engine as Google Chrome, to be faster, smaller and more efficient.
  3. Real-time updates — Got a room change? Or maybe a casual? It'll show up here (we make no claims as to the reliability of this information. Check your student portal or the room your class is timetabled at for more solid information)
All these good things, and if you're curious about how your data is handled, or want to contribute, you can take a look at the code which can be found here.

I'm logged out, but it still remembers my timetable?

That's normal. If you want to make it forget this you can either clear the local storage data in your browser or click here.
